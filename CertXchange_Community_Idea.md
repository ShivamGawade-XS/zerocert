# CertXchange Community Vision

## Purpose
Build a trusted community where learners discover high-quality certificates, learning opportunities, hackathons, internships, fellowships, and career resources.

## Principles
- Quality over quantity
- Verified opportunities
- Networking and peer learning
- Career-focused discussions
- Community reviews of certificates

## WhatsApp Structure
1. Announcements (admin only)
2. Networking & Discussions
3. Certificates & Opportunities

## Long-term Goal
Become the most trusted learning and opportunity community, feeding users into the CertXchange platform.
